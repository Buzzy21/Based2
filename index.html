Enter the prompts in the form of A#B#C... (such as 2+2, 3^5^1*5, etc.). Note that whitespaces are ignored.

Currently, the only supported operations are:
+ : Addition
- : Subtraction
* : Multiplication
/ : Division
> : Exponentiation
^ : XOR/Exclusive or
& : AND
| : Or
% : Modulus
_ : Floor
~ : Average
The order of operation does not apply.
